Welcome to the game Alchmey.
To play is very simple, the game will randomly generate a symbol for you to place on the board. 
You can only place symbols next to either a wild, or next to symbols that are the same in symbol
 or in color.

An 'X' denotes a skull and cross bones. You can place this over an exsisting symbol and it will
leave the square gold with a blue X. However you can place any symbol over the X sqaure.

To Discard, set the x and y coordinates to be J and 1. 

An upside down ? denotes a wild. You can place this next to any valid symbol.

The objective of the game is make the entire board gold by placing symbols on the board. 
Four discards results in an end of game situation.s
